📋 How the System Works

🔒Days 1–5: Only Base habits + 1 compulsory habit. Build the foundation first.
🔓Day 6: Your first Life Path habit unlocks automatically.
📈Every 10 days: Complete 3 out of 5 habits for 10 days → earn 1 new habit slot from any domain.
✅Always compulsory: "Prepare for next day before sleep" — never skippable.
🎯Phase 1 selection: 1 compulsory + 2 base + 2 universal + 1 custom (optional) = max 5 habits.
